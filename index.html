About

Stuart, Donna, Ian and Andy — a four-piece from Falkirk writing moody, unpolished pop built for the room, not the algorithm.

Formed in late 2025, drawing from Frank Zappa, Mighty Mighty, The Close Lobsters and The Monkees. No socials, no clout-chasing — just rare, deliberate shows and word-of-mouth.

Their debut recordings were laid down in March 2026 — raw, live-to-room, with a debut EP on the way.
